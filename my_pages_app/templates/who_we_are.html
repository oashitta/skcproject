WHO WE ARE

Street Kids Can is a volunteer led organization committed to nurturing and unlocking the skills and talents of street children through interactive and unconventional educational resources and activities to boost their self-esteem, empower them and provide an opportunity for them to break out of generational poverty and take charge of their lives for a brighter future.

Our Mission

To empower street children to find their path out of poverty by nurturing their strengths and talents through informal educational activities.

Our Vision

We envision a future where every street child is equipped with skills and resources to break free from generational poverty and achieve sustainable development.

Our Target

By the year 2032, we aim to have impacted 10,000 street kids across Nigeria and successfully re-integrate them into the mainstream society armed with the necessary mental and vocational skills to thrive and live independently off the streets.

We envision our program will empower them and help them break away from generational poverty through provision of informal education and life skills that will prepare them for sustainable development and a brighter future.

Our Approach

We believe a positive self-image is at the core of street kidsâ€™ ability to successfully reintegrate into mainstream society. Unfortunately, the complex circumstances leading to these kids being on the streets often leaves them feeling unworthy.

As a result, our programs are designed to nurture and unlock the strengths and talents of each child to help them regain their self-esteem and hopes of a brighter future.

Our programs are designed to facilitate self-discovery and nurture hidden talents through informal education and recreational activities. Children in our programs will spend time each week participating in activities varying from mobile learning to therapy and counselling sessions as well as vocational and skills acquisition programs based on their individual interests.

Our volunteers and partners will dedicate time each week to facilitate the programs alongside our public and private sector partners who provide us with access to their spaces and resources.
